SF-Sense

San Francisco is a big city


and just like in every big city, bad things happens.

You want to be aware of the various incidents that occured around you, so you can make proper decisions.

SF Sense is here for you


a mobile application who let's you know where SFPD incidents has been reported.

Just pop it and you will instantly have an overviews of anything that happenend around you the past 3 months.

Or simply left your phone in your pocket, and you'll get a buzz notification anytime you walk trough an unsafe area
