SF Sense

SF Sense is here for you:

A mobile application that let's you know where San Francisco crime incidents have been reported.

Simply leave your phone in your pocket, and you'll get a buzz notification anytime you enter an unsafe area.

Heading to a new part of town? Check SF-Sense and you'll instantly have an overview of all crimes reported around you over the past 3 months.
